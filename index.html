Solar-system

This project illustrates a simplified model of the solar system using HTML and CSS animations. Here's a breakdown of the elements:
  • The central body, symbolizing the Sun, is depicted as a yellow sphere with a radiant glow effect created using a box-shadow.
  • Orbiting around the Sun is a blue sphere representing the Earth. The Earth rotates around its axis while simultaneously orbiting around the Sun.
  • A smaller white sphere, symbolizing the Moon, orbits around the Earth. The Moon also rotates around its axis while orbiting the Earth.
  • The animation demonstrates the Earth's rotational motion around the Sun and the Moon's orbit around the Earth, creating an interactive visualization of celestial movements.
